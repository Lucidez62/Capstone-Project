How Encryption-Based Steganography Works

This application combines the power of modern cryptography with steganography techniques to provide secure communication.

The Process:

  1. Encryption: Your message is first encrypted using your chosen algorithm and key
  2. Embedding: The encrypted data is then hidden within the pixels of your cover image
  3. Extraction: To read the message, the recipient needs both the stego image and your secret key
  4. Decryption: After extraction, the encrypted data is decrypted using your key

Security Features:

  • Multi-layer Security: Even if someone suspects steganography, they still need to break the encryption
  • Advanced Algorithms: Uses state-of-the-art cryptographic standards
  • Multiple Embedding Methods: Choose from different steganographic techniques for optimal security
  • Client-side Processing: Your data never leaves your browser for maximum privacy

Strong Encryption

AES-256, Twofish, and Serpent encryption algorithms to secure your messages
